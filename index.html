my question

Is there a correlation between buying stroller and babies gross motor skill development?

my hypothesis

  • If we put a lot of value in the stroller
  • Then the baby will spend more time restrained and will not be able to drive their gross motor development
  • Because habits and patterns are established early in life it is important to focus on physical activity instead of restraining objects such as strollers

my survey was made with surveymonkey

84 parents answered 3 questions:

  • How old was your oldest child when it started to walk?
  • How much did the first stroller you bought for your child cost?
  • How many children have you got?
